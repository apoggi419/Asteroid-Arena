Asteroid Arena

by Alejandro Poggi

How long can you last in the arena?

Instructions

Controls

Movement: WASD
Power-Up ability: E

Objective

Stay alive for as long as possible. You do this by not hitting asteroids and not running out of fuel.

Fuel

Your fuel bar will continuously run out as time progresses. This can be replenished with fuel.

Power-Ups

Quantum Blast
A powerful blast that obliterates asteroids in the area. It is the most powerful of your abilities, but also the most unpredictable.
Atomic Shrink
A unique technology that shrinks all of the asteroids on the screen. If shrunken enough, the asteroids will be destroyed.
Particle Slow
This spacetime warping tech will slow all of the asteroids on the screen, allowing you to maneuver with ease.
